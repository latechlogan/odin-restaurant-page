Welcome to Bloom & Brew Café — where every cup of coffee comes with a side of comfort, and every pastry is baked with care. Whether you’re here for your morning pick-me-up, a sweet treat, or just a cozy place to unwind, we’ve got a seat waiting for you.

Our Story

Bloom & Brew Café started with one mom’s mission: to make birthday cakes her daughter could actually enjoy. After too many parties with desserts off-limits, the kitchen turned into a lab of allergen-friendly experiments. Soon, friends and neighbors were asking for cakes too. And because coffee and cake are a perfect match, the bakery blossomed into a café.

Today, Bloom & Brew is still family-run, still passionate about delicious food, and still committed to creating a place where everyone can share in the joy of a treat.
